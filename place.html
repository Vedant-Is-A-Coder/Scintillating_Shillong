Hotels in Shillong

Shillong is one of the few states that has good hotels that give you an amazing experience. Below I have listed out 3 great hotels that you can stay at.



A La Maison

A La Maison is one of the only five star hotels that one can find in Shillong. It is located 15 minutes away from the police bazar and is an absolute beauty. They also offer free wifi and everything else that a tourist needs.



Da Blanche Inn

Located away from the city of Shillong in a quiet hilly-part of Shillong. This is the perfect place to stay if you want to stay close to nature. Its restraunt serves Chinese Cuisine and also has free wifi and a flat T.V screen.



Sha Ri Loum

A cozy and beautiful place to live, Sha Ri Loum is a place located in the hills of Shillong. It is an entire bungalow that you can have for yourself and it can fit up to 8 guests.
