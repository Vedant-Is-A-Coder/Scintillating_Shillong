Why Shillong

Once in a while, it is good to escape the busy metropolitans of India and get out into the wild, even while being in a city. That is the kind of experience Shillong, the capital of the North-Eastern state Meghalaya, offers. A land rich in flora and fauna, this is the perfect place to spend your holidays and get away from the noise and pollution of urban life. Known as the 'Scotland of the East', Shillong is also a place with a rich culture and heritage. This website showcases this city because many of us still do not know enough about the hidden paradises in North-eastern India. With the right kind of promotion, Shillong has the potential to emerge as one of the best tourist haunts in this beautiful and diverse country of ours.
