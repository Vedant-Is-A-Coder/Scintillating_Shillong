Experience

Shillong is a diverse city that offers a range of cultural, culinary, adventure and musical experiences.





Culture

Soak in the rich ethnic culture that is deeply cherished.The main tribe is the Khasi who follow a matrilineal system. Other tribes include Garo and Jaintia.



Cuisine

Bite into a range of authentic North-Eastern cuisine and street food, including delicious momos, tungrymbai, jadoh, dohneiiong and chowmein



Music

Shillong, the Rock Capital of India is the home to some of the world's best music. A well known choir called the Shillong Chamber Choir is based here.



Adventure

One of the top destinations for adventure sports, Shillong offers a variety of fun activities. A few of them are trekking, golfing, river canyoning, mountain biking and cave exploration
